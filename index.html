Introducing BiasAlias: Your Tool for Detecting News Bias

BiasAlias is an innovative app that helps users identify bias in news articles by performing sentiment analysis. It provides polarity and subjectivity ratings to highlight the tone and potential bias of content. With two main features—inputting a URL of an article for analysis or performing a Google search on a keyword—BiasAlias offers insights on multiple articles, including an alternate opinion to provide a balanced view. Stay informed and critically evaluate media with BiasAlias.
